WHY I'M RUNNING


Our Constituency covers Mikang, Qua’an-Pan and Shendam Local Government Areas which is an Agricultural hub in Plateau State that brings people from every state to this area. We are a diverse people living in rural and urban areas. I will represent all the people of the constituency. I will bring integrity, truth, and unity back to our constituency. I will break barriers with the people and I will build the bridges needed to connect our people and our communities. I will move Mikang, Qua’an-Pan, Shendam constituency into the future by collaborating with government at all levels, traditional and religious leaders, women and youth groups and all stakeholders to bring peace and growth to our area. I will listen to the different voices, and we will move forward together. I am for unity. I am for the people.

MEET JOHN M. DAFAAN


John Moenwul Dafa’an, a seasoned administrator, public servant and community advocate, is the All Progressives Congress’ (APC) flag bearer for Mikang, Qua’an-Pan, Shendam House of Representatives election. John Dafaan was born in Shendam on the 3rd of May 1963. John spent his early childhood in Shendam, Gboko and Jos where he started his primary school at Our Lady of Fatima Primary School Jos, after which he proceeded to the famous Government Secondary School Kuru.
